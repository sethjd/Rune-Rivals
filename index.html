Field guide

How to Play

Drop pairs of runes and connect 3 or more of the same kind up, down, left, or right. Larger groups and chain reactions strike much harder.

Fire12 damage
WaterCleanse + pressure
EarthShield + pressure
AirClear tile + pressure
Lightning7 damage + 2 junk
Shadow3 delayed junk

Keyboard: arrows move and rotate, Space hard drops, C holds a piece, S casts Arcane Surge, and P pauses.

Touch: the centered controls below the boards include move, spin, soft drop, Hold, Drop, and Surge.
